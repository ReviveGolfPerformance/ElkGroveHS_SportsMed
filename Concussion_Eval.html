Graded Return-to-Play Protocol

Gate to begin: symptom severity score must be below 5 at rest before Stage 1.

Record symptoms PRE (before activity) and POST (after activity) at each stage. Allow the athlete up to 30 minutes after the activity before the POST check, so any exertion spike has a chance to settle. A stage clears only when POST does not exceed PRE — no symptom increase. Keep stages at least 24 hours apart; if symptoms rise, stop, rest 24 hours, and repeat the stage. Full clearance at Stage 5 requires the athlete to be symptom-free (0).

—

Progression logic reflects the below-5 start gate, the rule that POST must not exceed PRE at any stage, the 30-minute post-activity settling window, and symptom-free (0) required for full clearance. Final return-to-play clearance after Stage 5 requires physician sign-off and must follow your district and Illinois state protocol.

Decision-support aid for a certified athletic trainer, not a diagnostic instrument.
